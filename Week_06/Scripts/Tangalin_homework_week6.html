Summary of Seasonal Chemical Patterns in Maunalua Bay

  • SPRING samples show lower temperatures compared to FALL,
    especially in the Transition and Diffuse zones.
  • Silicate concentrations are higher in SPRING,
    with the most pronounced increase in the Transition zone.
  • Phosphate levels are slightly elevated in SPRING,
    particularly in the Transition zone, suggesting seasonal nutrient shifts.
  • pH and salinity remain relatively stable across seasons and zones,
    showing minimal seasonal variation.
